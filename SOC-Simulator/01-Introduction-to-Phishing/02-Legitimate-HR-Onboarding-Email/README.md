# Legitimate HR Onboarding Email Investigation

## Scenario Overview

This SOC Simulator exercise involved investigating an inbound email alert that was flagged because it contained an external link.

The email was sent from the HR onboarding platform and requested that a newly onboarded employee complete their onboarding profile.

## Investigation Process

The alert was reviewed to determine whether the email represented a phishing attempt or legitimate business communication.

The following checks were performed:

- Reviewed sender information
- Reviewed recipient details
- Examined email content and context
- Verified the purpose of the embedded URL
- Looked for phishing indicators such as spoofing, urgency, credential harvesting, or malicious attachments

## Findings

The email was determined to be legitimate.

The sender domain was associated with the organisation's onboarding platform, the content matched a normal employee onboarding process, and no malicious indicators were identified.

## Classification

False Positive

## Escalation

No

## Outcome

The alert was closed as a false positive after confirming the email was legitimate business communication.
