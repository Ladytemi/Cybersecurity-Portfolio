# Screenshot Evidence

This folder contains screenshots collected during the investigation of a phishing email impersonating Amazon delivery services.

Evidence includes:

1. Initial alert details showing suspicious sender and email content.
2. Alert queue showing successful closure of the investigated alert.
3. Incident report (Part 1) containing activity timeline, affected entities, and classification rationale.
4. Incident report (Part 2) containing escalation rationale and remediation recommendations.
5. Incident report (Part 3) containing indicators of compromise and final findings.

The screenshots support the conclusion that the email was a confirmed phishing attempt and was correctly classified as a True Positive.
