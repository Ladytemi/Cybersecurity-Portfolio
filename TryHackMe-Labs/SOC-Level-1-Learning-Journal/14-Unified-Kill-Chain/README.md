## Overview

This room introduces the Unified Kill Chain (UKC), a cybersecurity framework designed to combine and extend traditional attack lifecycle models.

The Unified Kill Chain helps defenders understand attacker behavior across the entire intrusion process, including initial access, persistence, lateral movement, command and control, and actions on objectives.

Unlike the traditional Cyber Kill Chain, the Unified Kill Chain provides deeper visibility into post-exploitation activities and attacker progression within a compromised environment.
